📖 Why I Built This

Hi, I'm Bharathkannan. Like many of you, I once searched for health insurance online. Within minutes, my phone was flooded with 20+ spam calls from agents — all trying to sell me something I barely understood.

I realized the problem: there's no place to genuinely compare plans without giving away your data to brokers. Every portal sells your details to insurance agents the moment you enter your phone number.

So I built InsuraLens — a tool that lets you see what's really covered, what's hidden in fine print, and what gets rejected during claims — without a single spam call. Your data stays on your device. No agents. No pressure.

I hope this helps you make a better decision. Stay informed, stay protected. 💙

— Bharathkannan (rbkannan)
